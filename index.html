Who I am

Hi there! My name is Charlotte, I'm a Web Designer and Front-End Developer based in Chicago, Illinois. I have been working professionally as a Front End Web Developer and UX/UI Designer for over two years. I have been creating websites (mobile and desktop) for my agency, and also in my free time for clients. Design has always been my forte. I am constantly creating, whether it be for work, websites/applications, my own living space, or cooking and baking. If I am not stuck on my computer, you can find me in a local coffee shop playing my Nintendo Switch or reading. I am a huge nerd.
